What's Offside?

If you’re watching the World Cup this month, you’ll certainly encounter the offside rule a few times. What's “offside” mean?

The offside rule in soccer is mostly about passing: in a nutshell, it says you can’t pass the ball ahead of the defense. More specifically, it says you cannot receive the ball or interfere with play if you are:

  • in the opposing team's half,
  • closer to the opposing team's goal line than the ball is, and
  • closer to the opposing team's goal line than all but one defender.
